BeeQuickr


BeeQuickr is a webapp that uses the ResponsiveVoice library to read text and convert it to speech. The twist is that every time the most common word in the corpus is used, it gets faster. Based on the popular meme "Bee Movie" (2007) that doubles in speed every time the word "bee" is used, BeeQuickr provides a fun, easy, family-friendly experience that will make you feel simple & clean tonight.
